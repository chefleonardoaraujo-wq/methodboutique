Equity & Intangible Assets

The greatest value of a modern company lies not in its building or inventory, but in its intelligence. However, tacit knowledge (that which exists only in the founder's mind) is worth zero in an audit. To generate Equity, knowledge must be transformed into Intellectual Assets.

At Boutique Method, we prepare your company to be worth more. We identify, code, and register your proprietary processes, creating an IP (Intellectual Property) backing that multiplies the business Valuation in investment rounds or Exit strategies.

Standard Company

Method-Driven Company

Possesses auditable and replicable processes. The "know-how" is a corporate asset, not a personal one. Market value is multiplied.
