Origin

Boutique Method IP Holding was born in Santa Maria, Rio Grande do Sul, from practical experience in high-complexity environments — where clarity is not a differentiator, it is an operational requirement.

From the beginning, it was structured with a national vision: to develop proprietary methods capable of converting knowledge into solid intellectual assets, applicable across different sectors, regions, and scales.

What started locally was conceived to operate nationwide — under the same standard of rigor, governance, and predictability that guides every decision of the holding.

We are not an agency.
We are not a common consultancy.
We are architects of legacy.
