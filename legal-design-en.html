Legal Design

Law is a product. We make it user-friendly.

Legal Design is not about making contracts "pretty". It is about functionality. It is the application of UX (User Experience) principles to the legal world to reduce friction, speed up deals, and eliminate ambiguity.

Methodology

The Triad

Visual Law

Using icons, timelines, and flowcharts to explain complex clauses. The brain processes images 60,000x faster than text.

Plain Language

Removing archaic jargon (Latin, excessive formality) to democratize understanding without losing legal validity.

UX Writing

Information architecture designed for the reader. We guide the eye to what matters, creating a fluid reading journey.
